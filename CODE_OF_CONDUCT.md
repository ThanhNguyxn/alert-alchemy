# Code of Conduct

We want **alert-alchemy** to be a welcoming, inclusive project for everyone.  
By participating, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct.

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent; ask questions before judging.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.
- Help others learn and contribute.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information.
- Sexualized language or unwanted advances.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by emailing:
- **security@yourdomain.com**

We will review reports and respond as quickly as possible.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may take appropriate action, including warnings or bans, to keep the community safe.
